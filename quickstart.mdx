## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

<Callout kind="info">

- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: Existing documentation files (Markdown, MDX, or plain text) to import

</Callout>

NexusChat works entirely in the browserâ€”no downloads required.

## Create Your NexusChat Account

Sign up in under a minute.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Homepage" icon="globe">
    Open your browser and go to [https://nexuschat.com](https://nexuschat.com).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Registration" icon="user-plus">
    Click **Sign Up** in the top right. Enter your email, create a password, and choose a workspace name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification link from NexusChat. Click it to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the site and log in with your credentials. You'll land on the dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Set Up Your Default Documentation Space

Your default space organizes all projects.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Workspace Settings" icon="settings">
    From the dashboard, click your workspace name in the top bar, then select **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Basics" icon="sliders">
    Set a description, add a custom domain if needed, and enable features like real-time collaboration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes" icon="check-circle">
    Click **Save**. Your space is now ready for documents.
  </Step>
</Steps>
