## Overview

Channels help organize your conversations by topic or team.

## How to Create One

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Channels Menu">
    Click the "+" icon next to "Channels" in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter channel details">
    - Name your channel (e.g., `project-alpha`)
    - Optional: Add a description
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set privacy">
    Choose "Public" (anyone can join) or "Private" (invite only).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create">
    Click "Create Channel" to finish.
  </Step>
</Steps>
