## Overview

To start using NexusChat:

1. Visit `https://app.nexuschat.com` and click "Sign Up".
2. Enter your email and set a password.
3. Verify your email address via the link sent to you.
4. Log in to your NexusChat dashboard.

## Next Steps

- Join a public channel or create your own.
- Invite teammates by email or sharing a link.
- Start messaging and collaborating instantly!

Need help? Contact support at `support@nexuschat.com`.
