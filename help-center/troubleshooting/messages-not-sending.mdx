---
title: Messages Not Sending
description: Quickly resolve message delivery issues in NexusChat.
---

## Symptoms

- Messages show as "Sending..." and never deliver
- Error: "Failed to send message"

## Possible Causes

- Unstable internet connection
- Server maintenance or downtime

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your connection">
    ````bash
    Try loading another website or refresh NexusChat.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log out and back in">
    Log out of NexusChat and sign in again to refresh your session.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Status Page">
    Visit `https://status.nexuschat.com` for outage updates.
  </Step>
</Steps>
